General Vocabulary

General vocabulary includes words that are commonly used in everyday language and cover a wide range of topics. These words are essential for effective communication and understanding in various situations.

Word Definition Gujarati Meaning Example Sentence
Contribute To give (something, such as time, money, knowledge) in order to help achieve or provide something યોગદાન આપવો She decided to contribute to the charity event by donating books.
Innovative Introducing or using new ideas, methods, or concepts નવીન The company is known for its innovative approach to product design.
Diverse Showing a great deal of variety; differing from one another વિવિધ The team is diverse, consisting of members from various cultural backgrounds.
Illuminate To provide light; to make something clear or easy to understand પ્રકાશ પ્રદાન કરવું The professor used diagrams to illuminate complex concepts during the lecture.
Accommodate To provide lodging or space for; to fit in with the wishes or needs of આવક આપવો The hotel can accommodate up to 200 guests for the conference.
Adapt To adjust or modify; to make suitable for a new use or purpose સરળ Living in a foreign country, she had to adapt to a new culture and lifestyle.
Eloquent Fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing શક્તિશાળી The president delivered an eloquent speech that moved the audience.
Foster To promote the growth or development of; to nurture બઢતું The organization aims to foster a sense of community among its members.
Hypothesize To form a hypothesis; to propose an explanation based on limited evidence અભિપ્રાય રખવો Scientists hypothesize that the observed phenomena may be explained by a new theory.
Ponder To think about carefully; to consider deeply ચિંતા કરવું He sat by the lake, pondering the meaning of life.
Pragmatic Dealing with things sensibly and realistically પ્રાગતાત્મક Her pragmatic approach to problem-solving made her an effective leader.
Ephemeral Lasting for a very short time અલ્પકાલિક The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.
Serendipity The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way સેરેન્ડિપિટી The discovery of penicillin was a result of serendipity.
Conundrum A confusing and difficult problem or question કદાચિત સુળઝેલો પ્રશ્ન Solving the conundrum required careful analysis of the data.
Confluence The junction of two rivers, especially rivers of approximately equal width સંગમ The confluence of cultures in the city creates a rich tapestry of traditions.
Convoluted Extremely complex and difficult to follow જટિલ The novel had a convoluted plot that kept readers engaged.
Quintessential Representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class એકમુક્ત The film is considered the quintessential romantic comedy.
Perseverance Steadfastness in doing something despite difficulty or delay in achieving success ધૈર્ય Her perseverance in the face of challenges led to her success.
Voracious Wanting or devouring great quantities of food અતિશય ભૂખો After the hike, they had a voracious appetite for a hearty meal.
Pinnacle The most successful point, culmination, or peak શિખર Receiving the award was the pinnacle of her career.
Nefarious Wicked, villainous, or infamous મહાકાવ્ય The nefarious activities of the criminal organization were exposed by the authorities.
Quagmire A soft, boggy area of land that gives way underfoot ગરાબ The explorers found themselves stuck in a quagmire in the swamp.
Esoteric Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with special knowledge ગુપ્ત The professor shared esoteric knowledge that was known only to a select few.
Exemplary Worthy of imitation; commendable ઉદાહરણ His exemplary behavior set a standard for others to follow.
Conscientious Wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly ચિંતાશીલ The conscientious student always completed assignments with diligence.
Empathetic Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another સહાનુભૂતિપૂર્વક Her empathetic nature made her a trusted friend in times of need.
Ubiquitous Present, appearing, or found everywhere સર્વત્ર હાજર Mobile phones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
Quixotic Extremely idealistic; unrealistic and impractical અકલપનીય His quixotic dreams of changing the world often faced harsh realities.
Resplendent Shining brilliantly; characterized by a glowing splendor આભાસયુક્ત The garden looked resplendent with colorful flowers in full bloom.
Opulent Rich and luxurious, often characterized by elaborate or ostentatious details સમ્પન્ન The opulent palace showcased the wealth and grandeur of the ruler.
Cacophony A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds શ્રુતિસંગમ The construction site was filled with the cacophony of drills and machinery.
Mellifluous (of a voice or words) Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear મધુર The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
Exacerbate Make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse વધારવું His careless remarks served to exacerbate the tension in the room.
Ubiquity The state of being everywhere at once સર્વત્ર હાજરી The ubiquity of social media has transformed the way we connect and communicate.
Obfuscate Render obscure, unclear, or unintelligible અળંકારી The politician tried to obfuscate the issue by providing vague answers.
Querulous Complaining in a whining manner શિકાયતી The querulous customer expressed dissatisfaction with the service.
