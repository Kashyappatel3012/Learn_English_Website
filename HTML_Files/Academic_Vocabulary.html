Academic Vocabulary

Academic vocabulary comprises words that are commonly used in educational settings, including classrooms, textbooks, and academic writing. Mastering academic vocabulary is crucial for success in academic endeavors.

Word Definition Gujarati Meaning
Hypothesis A testable explanation for a phenomenon; an educated guess અભિગમ
Abstract A summary or brief overview; not concrete or tangible સાર
Analyze To examine in detail, typically for the purpose of explanation or interpretation વિશ્લેષણ
Synthesize To combine or create a whole from various parts or elements સિન્થિસાઇઝ
Evaluate To assess or judge the quality, importance, or value of something મૂલ્યાંકન
Paradigm A typical example or pattern of something; a model પેરડાઇમ
Comprehensive Including or covering all elements or aspects; thorough સમગ્ર
Empirical Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic પ્રયોગાત્મક
Infer To deduce or conclude information from evidence and reasoning અનુમાન લગાવવું
Critique A detailed analysis and assessment of something, such as a literary work or a theory આલોચન
Correlate To have a mutual relationship or connection with another thing સંમાન્ય સંબંધ
Validity The quality of being logically or factually sound; the extent to which a test measures what it claims to measure માન્યતા
Deduce To derive a conclusion from reasoning; to infer અનુમાન લગાવવું
Criterion A principle or standard by which something may be judged or decided માનક
Postulate To suggest or assume the existence, fact, or truth of something પોઝ્ચુલેટ
Empathize To understand and share the feelings of another સહાનુભૂતિ
Integrate To combine or coordinate different elements into a whole એકીકૃત
Extrapolate To estimate or forecast based on existing information or trends બહિરાવર્તન
Connotation The emotional or cultural association of a word beyond its literal definition સાંગ્યા
Pragmatic Dealing with things sensibly and realistically પ્રાગતાત્મક
Retrospective Looking back on or dealing with past events or situations પૂર્વદર્શક
Exemplify To serve as a typical example or representation of something ઉદાહરણ
Cohesive Forming a united whole; well-integrated સંગઠિત
Conversely In an opposite or contrasting manner ઉલટાવાઈને
Dialectic The art or practice of arriving at the truth by using conversation or dialogue વાદવિવાદ
Discrepancy A lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more facts અંતર
Epistemology The branch of philosophy concerned with the nature and scope of knowledge પ્રમાણશાસ્ત્ર
Heuristic A practical approach or method for solving problems or making discoveries હ્યુરિસ્ટિક
Immutable Unchanging over time; unalterable અપરિવર્તનીય
Inductive Involving reasoning from specific facts or observations to a general principle પ્રત્યેકવાર્તાનું
Ineffable Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words અકથ્ય
Interpolate To insert or introduce between other elements or parts અંતર્ભૂત કરવું
Juxtapose To place or deal with close together for contrasting effect સમાનાંતર
Linguistics The scientific study of language and its structure ભાષાશાસ્ત્ર
Mitigate To make less severe, serious, or painful ઓળખવું
Obfuscate To deliberately make something unclear or difficult to understand સ્પષ્ટતા ન આપવું
Paradox A seemingly contradictory statement or situation that may reveal a deeper truth પરેક્ષાંત
Quantify To express or measure the quantity of something પરિમાણવાત
Rationalize To justify or explain an action or decision with logical reasoning તાર્કિકીકરણ
Sociology The study of society, its structure, and the patterns of relationships within a society સમાજશાસ્ત્ર
Teleology The explanation of phenomena in terms of the purpose they serve rather than by postulated causes ટેલિઓલોજી
Ubiquitous Present, appearing, or found everywhere સર્વત્ર હાજર
Validate To check or prove the validity or accuracy of something માન્યતા પરિક્ષણ
Weltanschauung A particular philosophy or view of life; worldview વેલ્ટાનશૌંગ
Xenophobia Dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries ક્ષુદ્રભાવના
Yearn To have an intense longing or desire તૃષ્ણા
Zenith The highest point reached by a celestial or other object; the peak શિખર
