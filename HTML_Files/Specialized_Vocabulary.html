Specialized Vocabulary

Specialized vocabulary includes terms used in specific fields or industries. These words are crucial for professionals working in specialized domains to communicate effectively within their respective fields.

Word Definition Gujarati Meaning
Biotechnology The use of biological processes, organisms, or systems to develop or create new products બાયોટેક્નોલોજી
Cryptocurrency A digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security ક્રિપ્ટોકરન્સી
Neuroscience The scientific study of the nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord ન્યુરોસાયન્સ
Astrophysics The branch of astronomy that deals with the physical properties and behavior of celestial bodies એસ્ટ્રોફિઝિક્સ
Nanomedicine The application of nanotechnology in medicine for diagnosis, treatment, and prevention નેનોમેડિસિન
Quantum Mechanics The branch of physics that deals with the behavior of very small particles, such as atoms and subatomic particles ક્વાંટમ મેકેનિક્સ
Genomic Sequencing The process of determining the order of DNA nucleotides in a genome જેનોમિક સીક્વેન્સિંગ
Robotics The interdisciplinary field involving the design, construction, and operation of robots રોબોટિક્સ
Geospatial Relating to or denoting data that is associated with a particular location on Earth જિયોસ્પેશિયલ
Pharmaceuticals Relating to drugs and medications used for medical treatment ફાર્માસ્યુટિકલ્સ
Renewable Energy Energy derived from sources that are naturally replenished, such as sunlight and wind નવીન ઊર્જા
Telecommunications The transmission of information over a distance using electronic means ટેલિકોમ્યુનિકેશન્સ
Biomechanics The study of the mechanical aspects of living organisms and their movement બાયોમેકેનિક્સ
Chemoinformatics The use of computer and informational techniques to manage chemical data કેમોઇન્ફોર્મેટિક્સ
Hydroponics A method of growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent હાઇડ્રોપોનિક્સ
Entomology The scientific study of insects એન્ટોમોલોજી
Immunotherapy A medical treatment that stimulates the immune system to fight diseases, including cancer ઇમ્યુનોથેરેપી
Meteorology The scientific study of the atmosphere, weather patterns, and climate મીટિયોરોલોજી
Prosthodontics The branch of dentistry dealing with the design and fitting of artificial replacements for teeth and other parts of the mouth પ્રોસ્થોડોન્ટિક્સ
Seismology The scientific study of earthquakes and the propagation of elastic waves through the Earth સાઇઝ્મોલોજી
Genetic Engineering The direct manipulation of an organism's genes using biotechnology જીનેટિક ઇન્જિનિયરિંગ
Renewable Resources Natural resources that can be replenished naturally over time, such as sunlight and wind નવીન સાધનો
Artificial Intelligence The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems આર્ટિફિશિયલ ઇન્ટેલિજન્સ
Cybersecurity The practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks સાઇબરસુરક્ષા
Machine Learning A subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience મશીન લર્નિંગ
