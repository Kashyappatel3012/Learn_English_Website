Informal Vocabulary

Informal vocabulary consists of words commonly used in casual, everyday conversations. These words may not be suitable for formal or professional settings.

Word Definition Gujarati Meaning
Chill Relaxing or taking it easy શાંત
Hangout Spending time together casually સમય વાળો સાથ
Awesome Extremely good or impressive શાનદાર
Yolo Acronym for "You Only Live Once" એક જ વખત જીવો
Lit Exciting, full of energy ઉત્સાહપૂર્વક
Bummer Disappointing or unpleasant situation અચ્છા નથી
Grub Food, especially when it's good ખોરાક
Cheesy Unoriginal or inauthentic ફરમાયુક્ત
Coolio A cooler way of saying "cool" છોકરો
Fab Short for fabulous; fantastic શાનદાર
Munch Snack or eat in small quantities છોટા છોટાં ખોરાક
Chit-chat Casual and light conversation છેડછાડ
Gig A job, especially a temporary one કામ
Outta Short for "out of" બહાર થી
Sick Awesome or cool શાનદાર
Peeps People, friends લોકો
Couch potato A lazy person who spends a lot of time sitting on the couch watching TV આરામથી બેઠેલો રહેવો વાળો વ્યક્તિ
Freak out React with extreme emotion, often fear or anxiety ગબબવાડી કરવી
Buddy Friend or companion મિત્ર
Cram Studying intensely in a short amount of time બોર કરવું
Chow down Eat enthusiastically and quickly જલદીથી ખાવું
Kickback A relaxed, informal gathering આરામથી મિળકતી બેઠક
Stoked Excited or thrilled ઉત્સાહિત
Lame Uncool or socially awkward અલગાર
Snap A quick, clever, or witty remark તેજ
Heck yeah Expression of strong agreement or enthusiasm બિલકુલ
Wack Strange or crazy અજગર
Cool beans Expression of approval or agreement શાનદાર
