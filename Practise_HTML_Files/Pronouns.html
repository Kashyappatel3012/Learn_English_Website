Pronouns

1. Pronouns take the place of:

A. Noun
B. Verb
C. Adjective
D. Adverb

2. What do pronouns help us add to our speech and writing?

A. Personality
B. Variety
C. Detail
D. All of the above

3. What sort of pronouns are the words "he", "me", and "you"?

A. Interrogative
B. Possessive
C. Personal
D. Demonstrative

4. What sort of pronoun is the word "we"?

A. Singular
B. Subject
C. Object
D. Plural

5. Which personal pronoun is almost always used for a thing?

A. He
B. It
C. They
D. She

6. To refer to something that is far from you, use the pronoun:

A. This
B. That
C. These
D. Those

7. "This is Lisa speaking." In this sentence, what sort of pronoun is the word "This"?

A. Reciprocal
B. Demonstrative
C. Personal
D. Interrogative

8. Possessive pronouns can be subject pronouns or:

A. Object pronouns
B. Interrogative pronouns
C. Indefinite pronouns
D. All of the above

9. With a singular pronoun the verb should be:

A. Singular
B. Plural
C. Singular or plural
D. None of the above

10. What sort of pronoun are the words "each other" and "one another"?

A. Interrogative
B. Reciprocal
C. Indefinite
D. Demonstrative

11. What sort of pronoun is used to replace a specific person, place, or thing?

A. Indefinite
B. Demonstrative
C. Personal
D. Relative

12. Which pronoun is used to refer to a group of people or things?

A. Demonstrative
B. Personal
C. Indefinite
D. Reflexive

13. What is the pronoun in the sentence: "She gave herself a pat on the back"?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Interrogative

14. In the sentence "The book that you lent me is fascinating," what type of pronoun is "that"?

A. Relative
B. Demonstrative
C. Interrogative
D. Indefinite

15. Which pronoun is used to ask questions?

A. Indefinite
B. Demonstrative
C. Interrogative
D. Reflexive

16. What is the pronoun in the sentence: "Those are the cookies that I baked"?

A. Demonstrative
B. Relative
C. Personal
D. Reflexive

17. Which pronoun is used to emphasize a noun?

A. Indefinite
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Intensive

18. Which pronoun is used to refer to something mentioned earlier?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Relative

19. What is the pronoun in the sentence: "He couldn't believe what he saw"?

A. Demonstrative
B. Personal
C. Reflexive
D. Relative

20. In the sentence "Whose book is this?" what type of pronoun is "whose"?

A. Demonstrative
B. Interrogative
C. Personal
D. Intensive

21. Which pronoun is used to show ownership?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Possessive
D. Relative

22. What is the pronoun in the sentence: "They found themselves lost in the forest"?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Interrogative

23. Which pronoun is used to introduce relative clauses?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Relative

24. What is the pronoun in the sentence: "Can you pass me that book?"?

A. Demonstrative
B. Personal
C. Reflexive
D. Interrogative

25. In the sentence "She and I will go together," what type of pronoun is "I"?

A. Personal
B. Demonstrative
C. Reflexive
D. Compound Personal
